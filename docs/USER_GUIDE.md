# AIR-DROW User Guide

> This guide applies to AIR-DROW 8.3.0 Export Preview & Save Polish Edition.

## Start a drawing

1. Open AIR-DROW.
2. Choose the brush or eraser from the top toolbar.
3. Draw directly on the canvas with touch, mouse or pen.
4. Use Undo and Redo whenever needed.

## Draw with your hand

1. Select **Hand** or **Camera**.
2. Read the camera message and allow access only when you are ready.
3. Keep one hand fully visible in good light.
4. Bring your fingers together, then move your hand to draw.
5. Hold up **two fingers** briefly to toggle Hand Eraser, then pinch to erase.
6. Hover your index finger over the top color palette briefly to select a color.

AIR-DROW does not start the camera until you choose Camera.

## Save a project

Open the Projects area, name the project if you wish, then choose Save. Your project stays in the current browser. Download a backup before moving to a new device.

## Preview and export artwork

1. In Draw settings, select PNG, JPG, WebP, SVG, PDF or AIR-DROW project.
2. Choose a preset, quality and Fit / Fill / Stretch layout.
3. Tap **Preview export** to inspect the output. This makes no download.
4. Close the preview or use **Export now** / **Share** only when you are satisfied.

AIR-DROW saves your current project before a real export. Camera composite is optional and only appears after you opened the camera yourself; UI controls and hand-guide lines are never included.

## Privacy

Your local drawing, settings and diagnostics remain on your device unless you explicitly export, share or use optional AI creation. Camera frames are not saved by AIR-DROW.
