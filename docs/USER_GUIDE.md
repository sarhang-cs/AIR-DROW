# AIR-DROW User Guide

> This guide applies to AIR-DROW 8.0.0 Final Release Readiness & Cache Integrity Edition

## Start a drawing

1. Open AIR-DROW.
2. Choose the brush or eraser from the top toolbar.
3. Draw directly on the canvas with touch, mouse or pen.
4. Use Undo and Redo whenever needed.

## Draw with your hand

1. Select **Hand** or **Camera**.
2. Read the camera message and allow access only when you are ready.
3. Keep one hand fully visible in good light.
4. Bring your fingers together, then move your hand to draw.
5. Hold up **two fingers** for a short moment to toggle Hand Eraser, then pinch to erase.
6. Move your index finger over the top color palette briefly to select a color. If tracking is lost, AIR-DROW safely holds or pauses the stroke.

AIR-DROW does not start the camera until you choose Camera.

## Save a project

Open the Projects area, name the project if you wish, then choose Save. Your project is stored in the current browser. Download a backup before moving to a new device.

## Export artwork

Open Export, choose PNG, JPG, WebP, SVG, PDF or AIR-DROW project. For image export choose 1×, 2× or 4× quality, a layout (Fit/Fill/Stretch), and a social or print preset. AIR-DROW saves the current project first so your newest changes are protected. Camera composite is optional and only appears if you opened the camera yourself; the UI is never included.

## Use App readiness

Open **Settings → About App → App readiness** when you want to confirm that local saving, graphics and AIR-DROW files are ready.

## Use Hand drawing check

Open Camera first. When the live camera is visible, open **Settings → About App → Hand drawing check** to see whether hand drawing is ready.

## Privacy

Your drawings and settings remain local unless you export, share or intentionally use optional AI creation. Camera video is not stored by AIR-DROW.


> **v8.0.0:** Added high-resolution export, hand palette/eraser gestures, adaptive smoothing and local diagnostics.


### Download safety

Hand tracking cannot start a download. Only a trusted physical activation of Save, Export or Share can begin a file action.
